Tactical Treehouse

About

Tactical Treehouse is a Discord bot that allows users to create private voice channels and text channels. Users can invite other users to their channels and have them join them. Users can also manage their channels and invite other users to them.

Features

  • Create private voice and text channels
  • Invite other users to your channels
  • Manage your channels and invite other users to them

Commands

  • !create-channel : Creates a private voice or text channel with the specified name.
  • !invite : Invites the specified user to your channel.
  • !kick : Kicks the specified user from your channel.
  • !ban : Bans the specified user from your channel.
  • !unban : Unbans the specified user from your channel.
  • !lock: Locks your channel.
  • !unlock: Unlocks your channel.
  • !rename : Renames your channel to the specified name.
  • !delete: Deletes your channel.
